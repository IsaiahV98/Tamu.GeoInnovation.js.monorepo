Texas A&M University — Aiding in the Fight Against COVID-19

Free Geocoding For The Public Health Community

Texas A&M Geoservices is fully prepared to continue supporting service operations during during the coronavirus COVID-19 disease pandemic. In addition, the Texas A&M University (TAMU) GeoInnovation Service Center has been authorized by TAMU to provide assistance to the national and international public health communities by providing free and unlimited access to our geocoding platform for COVID-19 public health-related surveillance, treatment, and control in the United States.

Geocoding in the Fight Against the COVID-19 Pandemic

The distribution of COVID-19 cases in the United States can be described geographically. Accurate geocoding is a valuable resource used to convert human-retable addresses into latitude and longitude pair values that can further be used for geo-spatial analysis.

Geocoding costs at scale quickly add up. Texas A&M is eager to facilitate the ongoing efforts by the public health community against the COVID-19 pandemic by eliminating the financial barrier.
